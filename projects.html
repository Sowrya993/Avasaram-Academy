Brain Tumor Detection using ResNet

This project leverages ResNet, a deep learning architecture renowned for image classification. Brain tumors present a significant medical challenge requiring timely and accurate diagnosis for treatment planning. Using ResNet's robust feature extraction capabilities, the system analyzes MRI scans to identify regions indicative of brain tumors.

By training on large datasets of diverse brain tumor cases, the model distinguishes between healthy brain tissue and tumor-afflicted regions with high precision, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and expediting treatment initiation.

Deep Learning CNN Diagnostics

Chest X-ray Analysis with CNN

This project explores Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for analyzing chest X-ray images for medical diagnosis. Chest X-rays serve as a crucial diagnostic tool for detecting various pulmonary conditions including pneumonia, tuberculosis, and lung cancer.

By training CNN models on extensive datasets of diverse chest X-ray images, the system accurately differentiates between healthy and diseased lung tissue. This investigation showcases deep learning's potential in revolutionizing chest X-ray analysis, offering clinicians valuable insights for timely and effective patient care.

TGRTC Bus Arrival Prediction

The TGRTC Bus Prediction project aims to predict whether students can reach their destination on time using RTC bus services. The project gathers data from RTC bus stops including bus schedules, routes, and environmental factors such as weather conditions and traffic patterns.

Leveraging machine learning models including Support Vector Machines (SVM), Decision Trees, and Naive Bayes classifiers, the system analyzes collected data to forecast student arrival likelihood. By integrating real-time and historical data, it provides valuable insights for students planning their commute.
